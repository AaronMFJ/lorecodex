In-app feedback

Send feedback from inside Lorecodex.

1

Open Settings

Tap the gear tab at the bottom right.

2

Scroll to "Feedback"

Type anything on your mind. Your message plus the app version, iOS version, and device model are sent — nothing else.

3

Hit send

Feedback goes to me through Apple CloudKit. There's no account; if you want a reply, leave your email in the message.

Before you write

Quick checks.

iCloud not syncing? Open iOS Settings → your Apple ID → iCloud → Apps Using iCloud, and make sure Lorecodex is on. Sync can take a minute after a big import.
Voice capture misreads? Voice-added cards are marked with an asterisk. Open Recents, tap the failed row to fix the set/card.
Importing from Dreamborn? Export as Dreamborn Bulk CSV. Lorecodex rebuilds counts and foils from the file as-is.
