SESSION MANAGER

New Set? Record cards and export just the new pulls.

Sessions are a powerful way to add to cards to collection and export Dreamborn without a full collection push. Just the new cards.

  • 1Open app and start a Session.
  • 2Log your cards. They land in your collection and the session's own tally.
  • 3End it and export only those cards to Dreamborn or Lorcana.gg. Sessions can be edited, renamed, exported, resumed, or deleted.
a session running on the capture screen + session viewer
Caught something wrong?

Every entry stays editable after the fact.

Misheard a card or fat-fingered a number? Recents keeps the raw capture, flags anything it couldn't match, and lets you fix, retry, or delete it right where it landed, so you always know exactly where the mistake was.
