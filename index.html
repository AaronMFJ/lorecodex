Features

Built for the mess of a new release.

Stacks of unsorted cards, a pile of hot singles, and no time to fuss. Here's what Lorecodex does to help.

Session mode

Export just the new pulls — don't duplicate your collection.

Start a Session when you crack a box. New cards go into your permanent collection and into a session bundle. Export the session on its own to Dreamborn — clean, no overlap.

Voice capture · beta

Say it — "set 4, card 188, foil" — and it's logged.

Hands full of cards? Call them out. Speech runs 100% on-device. Voice-added cards are marked with an asterisk so you can spot-check later.

"Set four, card one eighty-eight, foil"
✓ On-device Marked * for review
Quick entry

Set pad + card pad.

Two separate selectors so you don't need to pre-sort. Pick a set once, thumb card numbers. Normal or foil, one tap.

Recents

See every capture land.

Successes and failures appear inline in the order you added them. Misread a voice capture? Tap to fix it.

Browse & collection

Every released card, every view.

Browse the full catalogue — sets, starter decks, promos. Collection browser supports multiple views and filters.

Quietly private

Private by nature, not by policy.

Your collection lives on your device. Voice recognition runs on your device. No account, no analytics, no third-party SDKs. There's almost nothing to say about the data — which is the point.
